I’m a Robotics Research Associate at IIT Madras working on multi-agent systems, estimation, motion planning, and real robot deployments. My work is “deployment-first”: I care about coordination and decision-making that survives sensing noise, timing mismatch, and safety constraints—especially in multi-robot scenarios.

Research Interests

My goal is to build reliable multi-robot autonomy under uncertainty. I’m particularly interested in multi-agent motion planning (task allocation, MAPF/CBS-style coordination), estimation (EKF fusion, frame alignment, outlier rejection), and game-theoretic robotics (Attacker–Target–Defender interactions). I care about methods that remain stable when deployed on real robots with imperfect sensing and actuation.
