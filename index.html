About

I’m Harish Bhamitipadi Magesh (Harish BM), a Robotics Research Associate working on multi-agent systems, motion planning, estimation, and control — with an emphasis on real deployments and reproducible experiments.

I’ve built and stabilized multi-robot pipelines on TurtleBot fleets: synchronized communication, repeatable SLAM + navigation, and experiment logging. My recent work focuses on game-theoretic multi-agent interactions (Attacker–Target–Defender), and on closing the gap between interaction models and what real robots actually do under imperfect sensing/actuation.

Current: Game Theory & Multi-Agent Systems Lab, IIT Madras (Research Associate).
Previous: Biorobotics Lab, Carnegie Mellon University (Research Intern); Mechatronics Lab, SASTRA University.
