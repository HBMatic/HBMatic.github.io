Research Interests

My research goal is to develop robust multi-robot autonomy under uncertainty, focusing on: multi-agent coordination (task allocation, MAPF/CBS-style constraints), motion planning (A*, Dijkstra, routing), and estimation (EKF fusion, frame alignment, outlier rejection) to maintain global-frame consistency across robots. I’m also interested in differential games (Attacker–Target–Defender) and how real-world sensing/actuation imperfections shift outcomes.
